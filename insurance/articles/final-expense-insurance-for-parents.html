Final Expense Insurance for Parents

This guide is for people researching final expense and trying to understand what protection may fit their family, home, or retirement situation.

What to know first

Insurance decisions usually depend on age, health, state, budget, current coverage, and what financial risk you are trying to protect.

Questions to ask

  • Who would be financially affected if something happened?
  • What monthly budget is realistic?
  • Do you need temporary or permanent coverage?
  • Are you protecting a mortgage, burial cost, income, spouse, kids, or retirement plan?
  • Do you already have coverage that needs review?
